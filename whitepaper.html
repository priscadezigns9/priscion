I. Executive Summary

Prisicon is a specialized Layer 1 blockchain built on the Cardano settlement layer. It is designed to act as the sovereign rails for the transition of elite Web2 consumer brands into a high-fidelity Web3 ledger environment.

II. The Cardano L1 Advantage

Unlike secondary "apps" or L2 sidechains, Prisicon operates as a foundational network. This provides direct security inherited from Cardano’s peer-reviewed Ouroboros consensus mechanism.

Native Asset Fidelity

$PRN (Prisicon) tokens are Native Assets on the Cardano ledger. They require no smart-contract overhead for basic transfers, ensuring maximum speed and minimal transaction costs for the ecosystem.

III. The .pri Identity Protocol

Identity is the cornerstone of the Prisicon network. The .pri handle system creates a permanent, on-chain identifier for every user, enabling seamless cross-brand interaction across the node network.

IV. Sovereign Migration Architecture

Prisicon provides the infrastructure for Web2 entities to digitize their inventory, reputation, and identity. By bridging legacy databases to the Cardano ledger, we ensure that the "Ledger Never Lies."
