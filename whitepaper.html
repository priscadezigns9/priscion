3. Migration Services

Sovereign Onboarding

This entry-level service focuses on identity engineering. It includes the provision of a primary .pri handle, the generation of a Sovereign Vault key, and the mapping of personal brand DNA to the ledger. This ensures that a user’s digital presence is cryptographically owned and immutable from day one.

A deep-tissue architectural service for corporations. This involves "Heritage Anchoring"—the process of moving legacy Web2 databases, inventory systems, and contract logic into the Priscion L1. This allows businesses to operate with 100% transparency and near-zero latency, using the blockchain as their primary server infrastructure.

4. Jello Layer & Lynx Privacy

Privacy on Priscion is powered by the Jello Layer—a zero-knowledge encryption protocol that allows users to prove identity without revealing private data. Lynx Messenger operates on this layer, ensuring that peer-to-peer and agent-to-user communication is entirely sovereign and invisible to third-party observers. It is a "Dark Signal" channel, meaning no metadata is leaked to the public ledger.

5. Global Legal Alignment

Sovereign Compliance Framework

Priscion is engineered to align with global regulatory standards while preserving decentralization:

  • MiCA & VASP Ready: Our architecture supports regulated asset classifications to ensure seamless integration with EU and International markets.
  • Data Sovereignty (GDPR/CCPA): By placing data control in the user's MUSE Wallet, we comply with the "Right to be Forgotten" through cryptographic revocation.
  • Analytical Transparency: While transactions are private via Jello, the ledger remains auditable for AML/KYC purposes through "view keys" provided only by the owner.

Our legal strategy ensures that Priscion remains a legitimate, high-fidelity business environment for global corporations and individual evangelists alike.
