The Jello Privacy Layer

Jello is Priscion’s native zero-knowledge privacy protocol. It ensures that while transactions are verified on-chain, the "metadata" of the high-fidelity assets remains encrypted and visible only to the authorized handle ($username.pri).

2. Heritage Preservation

Heritage Preservation refers to the **Cryptographic Anchoring** of legacy business data. When a brand migrates to Priscion, we don't just move files; we "sign" their history. This process ensures that 100 years from now, the DNA of the original brand—its founding assets, legal origins, and high-fidelity history—remains verifiable and unalterable on the ledger.

3. Global Legal Alignment

Priscion aligns with international digital asset laws through **Sovereign Compliance Nodes**:

GDPR/Data Privacy: Via the Jello Layer, users maintain 100% ownership of their "Right to be Forgotten" by revoking ledger-access keys.

MiCA/Regulatory Frameworks: $MUSD is strictly a utility-backed stablecoin, ensuring it meets electronic money requirements by only minting upon real-world service acquisition.
