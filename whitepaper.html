2. Consensus: Proof of Sovereignty (PoS)

Unlike Proof of Work (Power-heavy) or standard Proof of Stake (Capital-heavy), Priscion utilizes Proof of Sovereignty. This consensus mechanism prioritizes "Anchored Identity Nodes." Validators are required to maintain a high-fidelity $PRN stake and a verified .pri identity. This ensures that the network is secured by the stakeholders of the Empire itself.
