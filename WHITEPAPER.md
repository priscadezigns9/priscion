# THE PRISCION WHITE PAPER (v1.3)
## Vision: The Great Migration (Web2 to Web3)

### 1. The Core Thesis
Priscion is the **Sovereign Layer** for the next generation of commerce. The mission is to bridge the gap between traditional Web2 business models and the decentralized autonomy of Web3. 

### 2. The Multi-Token Ecosystem
The Priscion Network operates on a specialized multi-token economy, ensuring liquidity, governance, and privacy.

*   **$PRN (Priscion):** The primary utility token. Used for public network fees, brand activations, and **Public NFTs** (Brand assets, collectibles, and marketplace items).
*   **$NRL (Neural):** The private reserve and governance asset. Used for **Neural Login** authentication and **DAO Governance**. $NRL holders vote on charity distributions from the Impact Fund.
*   **$JLO (Chillata/Jello):** The privacy layer token. Powers the **Chillata Privacy Protocol**. Used for **Private NFTs** (Digital IDs, Deeds, sensitive documents) and anonymous transactions.
*   **$MUSD (Muze USD):** The ecosystem’s stable settlement token. Ensures price stability for commerce and merchant payouts.

### 3. The MUZE Wallet & Integrated DApps
MUZE is more than a wallet; it is a gateway to the Priscion ecosystem. It features a deep-integrated DApp suite:

*   **LYNX (Messenger):** A handle-to-handle, peer-to-peer encrypted messenger. No phone numbers required—only your sovereign .pri handle.
*   **MYNT (Marketplace & Mint):** The unified store for assets. Users can mint anything into an NFT—from an Elite ID card to a property deed.
    *   **Public Minting:** Uses $PRN. Visible to the network.
    *   **Private Minting:** Uses $JLO. Encrypted via the privacy layer, visible only in the user's Vault.
*   **PULSE (Explorer):** Real-time network intelligence and activity tracking.
*   **LEGGO:** The ecosystem builder/launchpad for new sovereign brand nodes.

### 4. On-Chain Identity & Private Vaults
*   **Sovereign Handles:** Identity is anchored to .pri handles.
*   **The Vault:** A high-fidelity storage layer within MUZE where Private NFTs ($JLO) and sensitive assets are held securely.
*   **Neural Login:** A Web2-style sliding modal that uses OIDC/DID to provide a "password-less" yet secure entry into the Web3 world.

### 5. The Share-to-Earn Engine (Phase 1)
The primary entry point for the network.
*   **Community Growth:** Turning the audience into a global marketing force.
*   **Incentivized Sharing:** Users share brand content and earn rewards, introducing them to the MUZE Wallet.
*   **Foundation First:** This phase builds the user base (the "Members") before full business integration.

### 6. Implementation Roadmap
*   **Phase 1: The Foundation.** Launch MUZE Wallet, $PRN/$NRL/$JLO tokens, and the Share-to-Earn engine. Deploy LYNX and MYNT core.
*   **Phase 2: The Migration.** Integrate established network brands once the ecosystem is populated and fluid.

### 7. The Charity Hub (DAO Powered)
*   **Independent Partner:** The Way Made Known (TWMK) is an independent NGO and primary charitable partner.
*   **Invisible Impact:** A 10% fee is built into product pricing, ensuring the mission is funded effortlessly without friction.
*   **Governance:** $NRL holders vote on distributions to Architect-selected charities.
